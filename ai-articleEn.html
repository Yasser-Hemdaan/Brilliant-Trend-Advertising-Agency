ChatGPT's competitor among Google's 14 updates

At the Google conference, many new products and updates for its various services were announced, with a focus on enhancing these products and services with features that rely on Generative AI technologies. This includes Google Search, the chatbot competitor to ChatGPT called Bard, Gmail, and others. The conference also saw Google unveil its first smartphones.

Here are the most important updates from Google during the Google I/O 2023 conference:

  1. The chatbot Bard has become smarter.
  2. Google search supports artificial intelligence features.
  3. Artificial intelligence now supports image editing tools.
  4. Google Maps.
  5. Workspace suite has become AI-powered.
  6. Text-to-music conversion.
  7. Replying to messages with AI support.
  8. Find My Device supports different Android devices.
  9. The second version of the PaLM 2 artificial intelligence model.
  10. The first personal notebook with artificial intelligence.
  11. The unveiling of its first foldable smartphones, the Pixel Fold.
  12. Announcing the new version of the Pixel 7A smartphone.
  13. Launching the new Pixel Tablet.
  14. The new version of the Wear OS 4 smart watch.

The chatbot Bard has become smarter.

Bard chatbot, Google's competitor ChatGPT, is now available to all users in 180 countries around the world. This is without the need to register in the waiting list, and it also announced many new features for its chatbot, which is based on AI technologies; Including support for the Arabic language, in addition to support for exporting answers to Gmail and Google Docs directly.

Bard is now able to help build, explain, and debug code in more than 20 programming languages.

Google search support with artificial intelligence features:

Announced a new search experience through its famous Internet search engine with features based on Generative AI; Direct results or answers to search queries appear directly above the search results, with the ability to text chat or write more questions to reach more accurate results.

Artificial intelligence now supports photo editing tools:

Announcing a new Magic Editor tool in its Google Photos photo management app; It is a tool that allows you to intelligently modify images.

Google Maps:

Google revealed support for the new "Road View" feature for its Google Maps application in specific cities; Including details about simulated traffic, bike lanes, complex intersections, and parking lots.

Workspace gets an AI touch to make it even smarter:

Google announced Duet AI smart assistant in its Workspace services; To easily produce content based on artificial intelligence techniques, for example, request writing a job description for a job in Google Docs directly through a text request, or help writing e-mail, or creating spreadsheets in Sheets, or creating presentations in Slides, including the production of images Through text description and others.

Convert text to music:

Launching the MusicLM tool that turns text into music with the support of AI, and creates unique songs based on your description.

Reply to messages with the support of artificial intelligence:

The Google Messages application has new features supported by AI technologies, including the Magic Compose feature that provides the ability to generate responses to messages that reach the user in an automatic way that fits the context of the conversation, in addition to a new feature for creating phone wallpapers based on artificial intelligence by describing the background text.

Find My Device supports different Android devices:

The "Find My Device" feature now supports finding lost Android devices, so that the feature now supports wireless earphones such as Pixel Buds Pro, smart wristwatches, and others.

Announcing the second version of the PaLM 2 artificial intelligence model:

a basic template for most of the AI features offered by Google; Thanks to its wide capabilities and capabilities, the Bard chatbot works as a competitor to chatgpt from OpenAI, and supports PaLM 2:

  1. Imagen template for creating images.
  2. Codey template to help develop applications by generating code.
  3. Chirp audio-to-text model supports 100 languages including Arabic.

The first personal notebook with artificial intelligence:

Announcing a new project called Tailwind, which is the first personal diary for users based on artificial intelligence that helps the user to search for information and generate texts via artificial intelligence on any topic he writes about based on the data provided by the user, whether the texts he records in his diary or documents which he keeps in his Google Drive account.

Unveiling its first Pixel Fold smartphones:

It comes with an internal foldable OLED screen measuring 7.6 inches and an external one measuring 5.8 inches. It also works with a Google Tensor G2 processor and 12 GB of RAM LPDDR5 and a storage capacity of 256 or 512, and it carries 5 cameras; Three main rear cameras, including a resolution of 48 megapixels, along with an UltraWide camera and another telephoto camera, both with a resolution of 10.8 megapixels, with a selfie camera built into the external screen with a resolution of 9.5 megapixels and an internal one built into the foldable screen with an accuracy of 8 megapixels, and the mobile is now available for pre-order at a price starting from $ 1,800, and Google said that it will provide the Pixel Watch smart watch for free when purchasing the phone for a limited time.

Announcing the new version of the Pixel 7A smartphone:

The cost of the new version is medium, and at the same time it provides most of the features of the leading Pixel 7 series phones, and the mobile is equipped with a Google Tensor G2 processor, a 6.1-inch screen with a resolution of 1080p, and a better camera compared to the previous generation Pixel 6A, and the mobile is now available for purchase at a starting price of $ 500.

Launching the new Pixel Tablet:

The device comes with a magnetic base, so that when you are not using it; You can recharge it wirelessly, and it doubles as a smart speaker like the Nest Hub; With options to control your smart home devices, it also has a microphone array similar to "Chromecast" command capture built-in, so you can cast to it from your phone or laptop.

The new version of the Wear OS 4 smart watch:

Google's annual conference on artificial intelligence witnessed the announcement of the new version of its smart watch, and improvements were added to battery consumption and gave users the ability to save and restore screen faces, in addition to better integration with other smart home devices such as the ability to control lighting and media.

Google also said that the famous WhatsApp application for instant messaging will be available for the first time on smart wristwatches, so that users can exchange messages, listen to voice messages and respond to them directly through their smart wristwatch.

And she added that the new version will be available for updating later this year 2023.

Finally, all these new products and features will invade our phones very soon. To facilitate our daily affairs and become an integral part of our daily and practical activities so that you cannot do without it.
